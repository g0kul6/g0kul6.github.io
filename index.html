Gokul Kannan

I am a final year student from National Institute of Technology Trichy, India. I am currently working as a Research Intern at the Medical Mechatronics Lab at The Chinese University of Hong Kong , under the guidance of Dr. Ren Hongliang , where my research focuses on vision-language models for medical multimodal-reasoning. Prior to this, I interned at IIIT-Delhi under where I worked under the supervision of Dr. Rajitha Prasad on Causal Inference and Deep Survival Analysis.Additionally, I am a member of Spider R&D a student-run independent research and development club at NITT.

My research focuses on computer vision and reinforcement learning with a specific focus on vision-language models, segmentation and embodied agents.

For any collaboration, feel free to reach me at gokulknms6@gmail.com
